About Me

Hey there! I'm Your Name, a digital illustrator based in City, Country. I've been drawing since I could hold a pencil, and discovered Procreate in 2020 — it completely changed the way I create.

My work spans character design, environmental concept art, and experimental abstract pieces. I draw inspiration from mythology, nature, and video games. When I'm not illustrating, you'll find me hiking or trying to beat my own high score.
